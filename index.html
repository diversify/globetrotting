THE IDEA

Listen your way around the globe! Each point on the map is linked to music that is unique to that city.
Choose a city, lean back and experience the beat of the streets.
Based on data from Every Place at Once by Glenn McDonald at The Echo Nest. Created at the Diversify hackaton at Spotify.
